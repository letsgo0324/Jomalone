HISTORY

Jo Malone London, a British perfume and lifestyle brand.

Understated style. Defined by a rather unconventional sense of sophistication. Jo Malone London is a British fragrance and lifestyle brand, with a contemporary point of view. Timeless and elegant but always infused with a charming dash of wit and whimsy. From the start, their colognes have been revolutionary in their elegant simplicity and pioneering in their use of ingredients. Jo Malone London has evolved to become synonymous with coveted scents and luxurious products for bath, body and home.
